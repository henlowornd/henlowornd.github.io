打印的书，破校服和灼人的阳光

> How would you proceed loving the world  
When your world is jammed in an inch or two?  
As Nero tried to describe, ahead of his time  
In the midst of unmatched spirits of rhyme  
Or maybe lead-enchanted wine

> How would you proceed screening the world  
When your world is sunk in figures of loom?  
As when the string stirs and bounces  
In the hands of whom we never knew  
Or maybe 'twas but you

> How would you conceal discerning the world  
When it crumbles and falls just opposite yours?  
As when paling words from your nails flow  
Into the space of whom you wound  
And present themselves a blur?

> And how would you manage to buy the world  
When it simply gifts you from inevitable gloom?  
As he might rush it out, take his time  
To perceive how everything swirls  
Into right, right and right

### 在奥斯提亚

> 我看到一个世界  
立柱，无神的眼控  
孔洞和浮雕  
旋转的天顶画  
在昏黄的不见天日里  
燃烧的水汽  
酒红与天青味的旧梦  
一艘船离开，带着青铜的未来  
台伯上游的一场雨

> 我看到一个世界  
迟暮，下垂的紫边  
前人的神庙，今人的凯旋门  
无眼的正义  
溺在没入水中的极乐  
那是一片铅黑色的蓝  
和没入水中的骰子
